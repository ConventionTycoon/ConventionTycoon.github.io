10/15 In the spirit of trying out all sorts of things without finishing other stuff first, I've made some models in 3D in Blender, to use as reference when I draw the 2D art by hand. So far I've got some stalls and...an alligator? What's that all about...?
Sep 2018 9/28 I’ve started developing the concept and character designs for an in-game franchise called “My Girlfriend Is A Computer Virus?!” Your guests will eventually be able to wear costumes, attend events, and buy merchandise related to this and other in-game franchises.
9/10 ★ Very basic character customization is working, although it hasn’t been integrated into the base game. So really I’ve just made a dress-up game. In which you can basically only change your hair. But man, can you do that!
9/9 I’ve finished making a few templates to help me draw character hair and clothing efficiently.
Aug 2018 8/28 ★ I’ve started another semester of college, which means development will slow down, but I’ll be learning a lot more about software development.
8/10 ★ I’ve finished a basic character walk animation in Unity. I've chosen to use motion tweens instead of hand-drawn frame-by-frame animation, in order to allow for lots of different animations for lots of different costumes.

YOUR music...in Convention Tycoon?!

If you are a musician, I would be honored to feature your music in my game!

All genres are appreciated! Depending on your style of music, your songs might...

  • Play at an in-game rave! (Sorry, "glow stick dance.")
  • Play from an arcade game, such as a rhythm game, dancing game, or retro arcade cabinet
  • Be a theme song for an in-game anime, movie, or video game
  • Play from speakers somewhere in the con
  • Be menu music
  • Play in promotional videos, with your approval of course!

Here are some of the benefits I can offer:

  • I'll draw you and make you a VIP character in the game!
  • Your name and social media info will be prominently displayed in-game and on the game's web site.
  • If you would like your music to be a theme song (such as for an in-game anime or video game), we can work together to design the thing! (YOU: "I want this to be a theme song for a sci-fi anime about cute slime girls based on different kinds of soda pop." ME: "WHEN CAN WE START?")


If you are interested, please contact conventiontycoon@gmail.com and we can get started~!
